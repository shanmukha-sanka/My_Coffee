### 📖 About

**Coffee Bloom** is a single-origin coffee shop, founded by **Shanmukha Sanka** in **Vijayawada, India**.
We source beans exclusively from **volcanic-soil growing regions** — Guatemala, Ethiopia, and Java — where mineral-rich ash gives every cup its character. Every batch is roasted in-house, in small drums, and tracked by hand from charge to drop.

<br>
